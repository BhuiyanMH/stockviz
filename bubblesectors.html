Notes!

The intent of this visual is to explore the stock evolution of several companies during the Covid-19 pandemic. The first part of the visual are the zoomable bubbles that form an index of the available companies.
After clicking on a specific company, the linechart below is updated illustrating its stock evolution.
Someone may notice that there are companies highlighted with green stroke. This means that for this company there are additional information available, namely news extracted from Twitter. Such news can be explored in the linechart and can also be hidden/shown by clicking on the corresponding button on the top left of the chart.
