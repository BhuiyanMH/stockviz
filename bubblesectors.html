By now, you should have a general understanding on how the pandemic stroke the markets around the world. If this is the case, we would like to take you one step further: why not inspecting the consequences that the most important companies around the world had to face? From the words of Albert Einstein:

In the midst of every crisis, lies great opportunity

Each white bubble represents one of the most important company in the stock market. Clicking on it will visualize its fluctuations during the pandemic. As you can easily spot, some companies are surrounded by a green circle. Such feature indicates that the company has also some news associated to specific dates. Are these news partially responsible for the fluctuation of the stock?

Most of all we value the verifiability and that is why you can always check the original article! Just hover on a news and click on the link right after the stock prices.
