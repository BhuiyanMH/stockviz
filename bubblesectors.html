Future work

The intent of this visual is to explore the stock evolution of several companies during the Covid-19 pandemic. The first part of the visual are the zoomable bubbles that form an index of the available companies. What is still missing is a line chart that will be plotted when a company is selected, showing its stock evolution.

Another feature that needs to be modified is the size of each bubble that represents a company. This size will be proportional to the current value of the company. Such a modification can lead to very big and very small bubbles and hence some of them will not be visible. To solve it, we will add a filter menu that will let the user choose the range of the values that will be displayed.

Bugs to be fixed

As for now, the name in the white bubbles sometime overlap. This bug will be fixed in the future.
