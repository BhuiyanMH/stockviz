Read me first

The intent of this visual is to explore the stock evolution of several companies during the Covid-19 pandemic.

Notes!

  1. The first part of the visual are the zoomable bubbles that form an index of the available companies.
  2. After clicking on a specific company, the linechart below is updated illustrating its stock evolution.
  3. Someone may notice that there are companies highlighted with green stroke. This means that for this company there are additional information available, namely news extracted from Twitter. Such news can be explored in the linechart and can also be hidden/shown by clicking on the corresponding button on the top left of the chart.
  4. If a company has many news it could be difficult to read the articles. You can use the datepickers to focus the chart on a specific timespan
  5. Once a vertical line is hovered, a small description of the event will be available inside the linechart. For more informations, right after the visualization there is a link to the original article
